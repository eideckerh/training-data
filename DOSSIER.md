### Session Details

| Session Type | Target Power/HR | Duration | Purpose |
|--------------|-----------------|----------|---------|
| VO2Max / Hard Intervals | Per TrainerRoad prescription | 60-90 min | Raise aerobic ceiling and support FTP growth |
| Endurance | Intervals.icu Z2 / aerobic HR | 1.5-4 h | Aerobic base and fatigue resistance |
| Sweetspot | Intervals.icu SS range | 60-120 min | FTP-oriented muscular endurance |
| Long Ride | Mostly endurance | 2.5-5 h | Durability and aerobic support |
| Recovery | Easy spin | 30-75 min | Promote recovery without adding meaningful load |

### Recovery Protocol

**Recovery Triggers (Auto-Deload):**
- HRV down materially from baseline: reduce intensity
- Resting HR elevated materially from baseline: reduce load
- Subjective fatigue clearly elevated: shorten or downgrade session
- Two or more triggers together: switch to recovery or rest

**Feel Scale:**

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Excellent |
| 2 | Good |
| 3 | Moderate fatigue |
| 4 | Fatigued |
| 5 | Exhausted |
